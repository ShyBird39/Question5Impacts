Vote NO on Ballot Question 5

Why This Ballot Initiative is Harmful

The proposed ballot initiative to eliminate tip credits would have severe negative consequences for restaurant employees, guests, communities and the state:

  • Restaurants will face increased payroll costs, forcing them to cut hours, lay people off, and raise prices.
  • Higher prices lead to decreased demand, lower earnings and further job losses.
  • Fewer staff leads to lower service quality, further impacting demand, earnings, and employment.

    • A recent study has shown that:

  • For every 1% increase in price, restaurants see a 1.56% decrease in demand.
  • For every 1% decrease in service quality, restaurants experience a 1.85% decrease in demand.
